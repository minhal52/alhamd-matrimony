About Us

Established on 10th August 2025, AL HAMD Matrimony is dedicated to helping Muslims find their ideal partners through a faith-based, ethical, and family-oriented approach. Our personalized matchmaking ensures compatibility based on values, deen, and community trust.

Located at #205, 2nd Floor, Business Park Building, Bejai, Mangalore, we strive to bring families together with dignity and professionalism.
